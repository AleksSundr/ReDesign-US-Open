US Open Fan Week Returns in 2022

During this weeklong tennis celebration leading up to the main draw, access to the grounds will be completely free. This means that, once again, fans will be able to attend, at no cost, the US Open Qualifying Tournament, in addition to other events including a Legends match and US Open player practice, as well as music and entertainment throughout the week.
